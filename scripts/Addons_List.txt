Addon Factory:
Based On Scramble Addon "author": Saidenka, Fork by meta-androcto
Enables Menu's Eveywhere. 
To use, most menu's can be visibly turned on/off. Once you hide the menu's you don't like, 
go into the Addon Factory Addons Preferences to hide the on off options & save your user settings.
You can go back & turn them on again at any time.
Note this addon it'self contains many many addons from Blender add curve/mesh & other areas. don't enable same addon if it's here.
